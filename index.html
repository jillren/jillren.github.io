Why I Choose Coaching

Plot twist: I was the skeptical one

I used to think coaching was... fluffy.

You know the type? All feelings and no strategy. All "how does that make you feel?" and no "here's what we do next."

Then I became a client.

"The conversations that changed me weren't about optimizing my productivity or fixing my strategy. They were about someone seeing me — really seeing me — and asking the questions I didn't know I needed to answer."

That's when I realized:

All my corporate experience, all the strategic thinking, all the problem-solving skills — they were just tools.

What actually creates transformation is connection. Understanding. The kind of conversation where you feel heard, not fixed.

So here I am, starting fresh in coaching.

Vulnerable? Yes.

Authentic? Absolutely.

Because this isn't about having all the answers — it's about helping you find yours.
