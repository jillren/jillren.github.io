Why I Choose Coaching

A personal journey toward meaningful connection

My path to coaching began with my own transformation as a client. After years of achieving professional milestones and navigating complex organizational challenges, I discovered something profound: the conversations that changed me most weren't about strategy or optimization—they were about being truly seen and understood.

"I realized that beneath all our secular pursuits lies a fundamental human need to be witnessed, to be heard, and to know that someone genuinely cares about our flourishing."

This revelation ignited something I couldn't ignore. While I'm grateful for the analytical skills and strategic thinking developed through my professional journey, what truly energizes me is creating space for the kind of deep conversations that honor both our ambitions and our humanity.

Starting fresh in coaching feels both vulnerable and authentic. It's a return to what has always been truest about me: an almost overwhelming desire to help others flourish, paired with a deep belief in the transformative power of compassionate attention.

Meet Jill

Where experience meets heart

My journey has taken me from the halls of Stanford to boardrooms in Silicon Valley, from startup environments to established institutions, and through both private sector innovation and public service. While these experiences shaped my understanding of systems and strategy, they also revealed something deeper: my greatest fulfillment comes from moments of genuine human connection.

As an INFP, I've always been drawn to the stories beneath the surface. Like the characters in "The Best of Youth"—my favorite film—I believe that compassion and understanding can transform not just individual lives, but entire communities. That Italian masterpiece taught me that our most profound growth often happens through the relationships we build and the care we extend to one another.

Beyond the professional achievements and secular milestones, what drives me is an deep desire to help others flourish. I believe in the power of deep conversations, in creating spaces where vulnerability is welcomed, and in approaching each person's journey with the reverence it deserves.
