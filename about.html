About Me

Get to know your coach and my approach to transformation

Hi, I'm Alex Johnson

Certified Life & Leadership Coach

With over 15 years of experience in executive leadership and personal development, I bring a unique blend of practical business acumen and transformative coaching techniques to help my clients achieve extraordinary results.

My journey into coaching began after I successfully navigated my own career transition from corporate executive to entrepreneur. Along the way, I discovered my passion for helping others unlock their potential and create lives of purpose and fulfillment.

I hold certifications from the International Coach Federation (ICF) and the Co-Active Training Institute, and I'm constantly expanding my knowledge through ongoing training in positive psychology, neuroscience, and leadership development.

My Coaching Approach

Client-Centered

You are the expert on your life. My role is to ask powerful questions, provide perspective, and help you discover your own solutions.

Results-Oriented

We focus on measurable outcomes and tangible progress, with clear milestones and accountability structures.

Holistic

True success encompasses all areas of life. We'll address professional goals alongside personal well-being.
